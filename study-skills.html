Excel High School now features BRYTE, a built-in AI tutor that offers personalized, 24/7 academic support across all subjects — helping you master concepts, stay on track, and succeed independently. Unlike schools that just provide textbooks, BRYTE delivers interactive help through our online platform, so parents don't have to be the tutor.
