FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my courses?
Follow the steps in your welcome email. You can also watch our LearnStage video guide here. If stuck, call us at 952-465-3700.
Can I use an iPad or phone?
We don't recommend it. Use a desktop or laptop with Google Chrome for the best experience. iPads and phones may distort the view and restrict quiz submissions.
Can I switch my classes?
Yes — within the first 30 days of enrollment. Submit a Help Desk ticket, contact your Success Coach, or call 952-465-3700.
How do I find my deadline?
Log into LearnStage and click on your program. Full-year = 12 months from enrollment; Semester = 6 months.
My deadline has passed and I'm not finished — what now?
You can purchase a two-month extension for $200. See extension instructions here.
Why is my account locked?
Two possible reasons:
  • Attendance Hold: No activity for 60+ days — submit a Help Desk ticket to unlock.
  • Declined Payment: Log into your student account, select your program, and restart payment installments.
I need help in a class — what do I do?
Submit a Help Desk ticket to reach an Academic Coach. Include the course name, module/assignment number, a description of the issue, and screenshots. This is the fastest way to get help.
My course shows 100% but isn't marked complete — why?
Every single item must be clicked — including the syllabus and surveys. Check your gradebook for anything missing a grade. Still not resolved? Submit a Help Desk ticket.
Have you received my transcripts from my previous school?
If you haven't received a confirmation email from the registrar, we likely haven't received them. Ask your school to email to records@excelhighschool.com. Processing takes 3–5 business days.
How do I know if I'm ready to graduate?
You need 21.5 credits, all requirements met, and your account paid in full. Submit a Help Desk ticket — you'll hear back within 2–4 business days from graduation@excelhighschool.com. Upload your ID and confirm your mailing address to avoid delays.
What do I receive when I graduate?
Each graduate receives a hardcover diploma and a congratulations letter. Make sure your mailing address on file is current so it ships to the right place.
How do I upload my ID?
Send a clear photo of your ID to graduation@excelhighschool.com.
